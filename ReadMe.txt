The first screen allows you to enter the names of tributes for all the districts. You must enter names into 
all of the textboxes in order to enter the simulation. When you enter the simulation, the events that occur
each day and a list of tributes are displayed. To advance in the simulation, click the "Next Day" button. 
At the end of the simulation, the winner will be displayed and you will have the option to close out of the
simulation or to start over.
